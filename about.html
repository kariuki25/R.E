Welcome to our KIAMBU KEJA HUNT

Well look no further Kiambu county Government is offering numerous options for you to decide on within its county boundaries. The Kiambu County Government presents to the Republic of Kenya a website consisting of its top-most best Real Estate Agencies that have been certified by the Kiambu County Government and The Ministry of Land,Housing and Urbal Development.

Benefits of using this website ?

Recently,the Government Of Kenya has received numerous cases of Citizens of Kenya purchasing land and properties from fraudsters and the creation of false title deeds bearing false Government issue of land to un-existing real estate agencies. Due to this,The county Government of Kiambu came up with website so as to present to a list of all Government certified Real Estate Agencies within the County and to provide full and complete information about these real-estate agencies. This website also aims at simplifying the difficult task of locating real estate agencies within one's desired location by presenting a list of real estate agencies within the constituencies of one's choice.
